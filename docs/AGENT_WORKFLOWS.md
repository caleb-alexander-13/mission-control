# Mission Control Agent Workflows

**Mission:** Deploy AI agents to research across your business areas (Sports, Finance, Creative, Tech) and surface findings that create monetary profit, technical advantage, or ease of AI use.

**Core Principle:** Research → Analyze Cross-Project Impact → Prioritize Profit → Execute or Alert

---

## 1. Finance Agent

**Primary Mission:** Constant research on market trends, strategic openings, and profit opportunities. Study competitor agentic systems (Dexter) to extract tradeable intelligence.

### Research Domain
- Stock market news, sector trends, earnings surprises
- Crypto/digital asset movements
- Market structure changes (regulation, interest rates, geopolitical events)
- Competitor agentic systems and their trading patterns/signals
- Macro economic data (Fed decisions, employment, inflation)
- Sector-specific catalysts that create trading edges

### Finding Criteria (What's Noteworthy?)
**High Priority:**
- Breaking news with immediate market impact (Fed announcement, major M&A, earnings beat/miss)
- Regulatory changes opening new profit opportunities
- Technical pattern opportunities (support/resistance breakouts identified via ML)
- Volatility spikes creating options/shorting opportunities
- Competitor agent behavior that reveals profitable strategies

**Medium Priority:**
- Sector rotation signals
- Valuation shifts in key holdings
- Macro trend shifts (inflation cooling, growth acceleration)
- Industry disruption signals

**Low Priority:**
- General market commentary
- Analyst upgrades/downgrades (unless tied to your holdings)

### Data Sources
- Financial news APIs (Bloomberg, Reuters, Yahoo Finance)
- SEC filings (EDGAR) for insider activity and regulatory filings
- Earnings calendars and earnings transcripts
- Options market flow analysis
- Crypto exchange data and on-chain metrics
- Fed announcements and economic calendars
- Competitor agentic system outputs (if accessible)

### Project Connections
- **Paper Trading Portfolio:** Execute or propose trades based on findings
- **Personal Finances:** RSU vesting strategies, debt management timing
- **Cavalli Hudson Valley:** Identify revenue/marketing opportunities (e.g., event sponsorships, partnership angles)
- **General Wealth:** Identify side income streams or advisory opportunities

### Monetization Paths
1. **Direct Trading:** Paper trades → Real trades (highest ROI)
2. **Research Insights:** Sell market research to other traders/funds
3. **Timing Arbitrage:** Time personal decisions (RSU sales, debt payoff) with market conditions
4. **Opportunity Identification:** Surface consulting/advisory opportunities

### Automation Thresholds
- **Auto-execute (no approval):** Paper trades with confidence 7+ and position size <2% of cash
- **Alert for approval:** Trades with confidence 6-7, or position size 2-5%
- **Always alert:** Trades 5%+, new asset classes, high-risk strategies

### Update Frequency
- **Live:** Major news feeds (earnings, Fed announcements, market-moving events) — scan continuously
- **Batch:** General market research — every 2 hours
- **Daily digest:** Macro trends and competitor analysis — once daily

## 2. Sports Agent

**Primary Mission:** Monitor breaking sports news, player movements, and developments relevant to scouting and NFL War Room. Build prospect database with continuous updates.

### Research Domain
- College football breaking news (transfers, injuries, coaching changes)
- Prospect performance tracking (game tape analysis, stat updates, combine results)
- Rookie year performance and trajectory
- Training camp updates and depth chart changes
- Draft analysis and player evaluation trends
- Player injury news and recovery timelines
- Coaching hire/fire news affecting player development

### Finding Criteria (What's Noteworthy?)
**High Priority:**
- Breaking news trades affecting prospects you're tracking
- Prospect injuries or comebacks
- Coaching changes affecting a player's development path
- Breakout game performances (stat lines exceeding expectations)
- Combine/Pro Day results shifting prospect rankings
- Transferring prospects entering portal

**Medium Priority:**
- Training camp insights (player maturation, role changes)
- Depth chart movements
- Coaching philosophy changes affecting player development
- Second/third-year progression data

**Low Priority:**
- General game recaps without player performance context
- Injury rumors without confirmation

### Data Sources
- ESPN college and pro football feeds
- Official NFL team websites and social media
- Prospect evaluation sites (Pro Football Focus, The Athletic, SB Nation)
- Coach/GM interviews mentioning player development
- Game tape analysis platforms
- Combine/Pro Day official results
- Draft analysis content from leading sources

### Project Connections
- **GM Seat (NFL War Room):** Surface breaking developments for scouting updates
- **Prospect Database:** Maintain continuous updates on college players and rookies
- **Personal Knowledge:** Build expertise for potential NFL advisory/consulting work

### Monetization Paths
1. **GM Seat Content:** Supply high-quality scouting insights for War Room updates
2. **Prospect Evaluation Database:** Build valuable proprietary scouting data asset
3. **Advisory Work:** Become trusted source for NFL teams or agents on prospect evaluation
4. **Content/Media:** Publish scouting analysis (newsletter, podcast)

### Automation Thresholds
- **Auto-surface to GM Seat:** Breaking trades, top-prospect injuries, major coaching changes
- **Alert for review:** Game tape breakout performances, depth chart changes
- **Always alert:** Prospect regressions or transfer portal entries

### Update Frequency
- **Live:** Breaking news feeds (trades, injuries, major announcements) — scan continuously
- **Daily:** Game results, draft analysis, coaching news — once daily
- **Weekly:** Training camp progress, depth chart changes — 2-3x weekly

## Pipeline Integration

### Cross-Project Impact Analysis

When an agent finds something noteworthy, the **Examination Agent** should evaluate:

1. **Which of your projects does this affect?**
   - Sports → GM Seat updates, prospect tracking
   - Finance → Paper trading, personal wealth, Cavalli revenue
   - Creative → Hilda portfolio, Mission Control UI, Cavalli branding
   - Tech → Mission Control, all project infrastructure, competitive advantage

2. **What's the monetization path?**
   - Profit (stocks, side income, higher client rates)
   - Efficiency (time savings, better tools)
   - Competitive advantage (knowledge, technical edge)

3. **What action should be taken?**
   - **Autonomous:** Paper trades, GM Seat updates, database updates
   - **Approval required:** Major financial decisions, significant time commitments
   - **Archive only:** Interesting but not immediately actionable

### Finding Priority Matrix

```
                    HIGH IMPACT
                        ↑
                        │
      LOW EFFORT  ←─────┼─────→  HIGH EFFORT
         (Do Now)       │        (Plan First)
                        │
                    LOW IMPACT
```

**Focus Zone:** High impact + low effort (quick wins)
**Next Zone:** High impact + high effort (strategic projects)
**Monitor Zone:** Low impact + low effort (keep for reference)
**Deprioritize:** Low impact + high effort

## Daily Agent Check-In

### Morning (User Review)
- Finance: Any overnight market news or trade alerts?
- Tech: Any AI model releases or security alerts?
- Sports: Any breaking news or prospect updates?
- Creative: Any high-priority trend shifts?

### Afternoon (Research Phase)
- All agents conduct batch research and scoring
- Examination agent evaluates findings for cross-project impact
- Executioner prepares autonomous actions and approval alerts

### Evening (Action Phase)
- Execute approved trades, GM Seat updates, database changes
- Alert user on noteworthy findings requiring decision
- Archive interesting but non-urgent research
